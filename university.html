Electrical Engineering

Requirements

  • Advanced Functions (MHF4U1)
  • Calculus and Vectors (MCV4U1)
  • Physics, Grade 12 (SPH4U1)
  • Chemistry, Grade 12 (SCH4U1)
  • English, Grade 12 (ENG4U1)

Why/Why not Waterloo?

  • Best-in-class co-op program—because no one will hire you without work experience these days
  • Lots of similar-minded individuals—I do lots of things that the average person wouldn't understand, so it would be nice to have more people to relate to
  • Intensive program—Because school will be a huge grind, I'll be accustomed to it by the time I hit work. They will keep you at work for a long time.
  • Lots of competition—Everyone wants to go to Waterloo at this school, so this lowers my chances
  • Intensive program—I will not have much social time because everyone will be studying.
